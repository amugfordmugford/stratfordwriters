Google Calendar goes here

To embed your group's Google Calendar, open Google Calendar, go to Settings → [Your Calendar] → Integrate calendar, and paste the <iframe> embed code in place of this block.

The iframe should look roughly like:
src="https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=..."

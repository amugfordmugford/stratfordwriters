Our Story

The Stratford Writers Collective is not so much its own group, but an attempt to bring together the various groups, events, and resources available to writers in and around Stratford, ON.

We believe that writing improves in community. A fresh pair of eyes catches what yours can't. A well-timed question unlocks a chapter that felt stuck for months. And knowing that others will read your pages next Tuesday is, frankly, the best motivation there is.
